PATHWAY FOR ENGINEERING SCIENCE STUDENTS TO GET A MINOR OR CERTIFICATE

Engineering Science students are welcome to pursue minors and certificates in the university. A minor or certificate is shown on your transcript, and the extra knowledge you learn will set you apart from other students. Due to the interdisciplinary nature of the program, you can earn some minors and certificates without taking a lot of extra classes.

DISCLAIMER: It is possible that the departments which offer the minors or certificates adjust the program requirements before we notice that. If there is any conflict between the schedule on this webpage and webpage specific for that minor/certificate, students should refer to that specific webpage and consult advisors for precise and correct information.

Note: Many courses have prerequisites. For some additional classes in the minor or certificate that are not included in the Engineering Science Program, it is possible that more classes are needed to meet the prerequisite requirements even that these classes themselves do not counted as required courses for that program. Students are suggested to take a closer look at the prerequisite requirements of the required classes of the program before committing to pursue it.
